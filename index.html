Our Mission

Who We Are

Built on Connection,
Trust & Impact

A consulting practice dedicated to strengthening Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander organisations across Australia.

Funding Connect Consulting was founded with one purpose — to ensure Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander organisations have the expert support they need to access funding and deliver programs that truly serve their communities.

We understand that funding is not just about dollars. It is about enabling self-determination, preserving culture, and creating change that is led by community, for community.

Our team brings together deep experience in grant writing, program design, stakeholder engagement and evaluation — all grounded in genuine respect for First Nations peoples and their ways of working.

Our Values

  • Self-Determination — Community voices lead every process.
  • Cultural Safety — Deep respect for Country and culture always.
  • Transparency — Honest, practical advice you can rely on.
  • Accountability — We answer to the communities we serve.
  • Long-term Thinking — Building capability, not dependency.

  • Connection

    Linking organisations with the right opportunities, partners and networks to maximise their reach and impact.

  • Trust

    Built through honest relationships, cultural respect and always putting community first in everything we do.

  • Impact

    Measurable outcomes that strengthen communities and support wellbeing across generations.

What We Do

Our Services

Specialist consulting designed around the unique needs and aspirations of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities.

  • Funding Identification

    We research and identify relevant government, philanthropic and corporate funding tailored to your organisation's goals and community priorities.

  • Grant Writing

    Expert writers craft compelling, community-led applications that articulate your vision powerfully and satisfy funder requirements.

  • Program Design

    We co-design culturally grounded programs alongside your community — evidence-informed, measurable and genuinely impactful.

  • Stakeholder Engagement

    Meaningful consultation and partnership development with government, community and sector stakeholders.

  • Monitoring & Evaluation

    Robust M&E frameworks that capture real impact and satisfy funder reporting — without the burden on your team.

  • Capacity Building

    Workshops, mentoring and tailored training so your team has the skills to thrive long after our engagement ends.
